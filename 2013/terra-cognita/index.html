Terra Cognita

2012-2013

As I moved to Berlin in 2012 I started tracking my first movements in the city, so that I could visualize the process itself of gradually discovering a new place.

The speed at which my coordinates move is mapped into different colors, so one can easily guess if I was walking (green), taking the train or a car (red), the tram (purple) or even riding a bike (blue).

The code is at https://github.com/jperals/terra-cognita.
