Wallball

2013

Wallball is a simple gesture-based game made with Box2D and Processing.

Draw lines which will become “walls”. These “walls” need to touch one ball of each colour (red, green and blue) in order to disappear. When a wall disappears it will give you points.

But be careful to not get the balls trapped between your walls and the top of the screen! When this happens the game will end.

Read more or just play.
